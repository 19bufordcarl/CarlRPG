# Antidote

When applied to a creature, it cures [Poisoned 1](../../../Game%20Procedures/Conditions/Poisoned.md). You must be [Engaged](../../../Game%20Procedures/Conditions/Engaged.md) with the creature to apply an antidote to them.

| 1                | 1      | 250   |
