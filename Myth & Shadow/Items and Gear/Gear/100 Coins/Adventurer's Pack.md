# Adventurer's Pack

**Every adventurer needs this basic gear.**

It comes with:

- A backpack
- A waterskin
- A coin pouch
- A bedroll

This gives you access to your [Stash](../../../Player%20Characters/Derived%20Statistics/Stash.md).

| ---------------- | ----- | ----- |
| 1                | --    | 100   |
