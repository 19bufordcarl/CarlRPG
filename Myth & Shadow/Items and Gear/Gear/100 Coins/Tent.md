# Tent

Holds all the PCs (~6). It is needed to sleep in the wilderness when there is bad or severe [Weather](../../../Game%20Procedures/Hazards/Weather.md).

*A [Wagon](../250%20Coins/Wagon.md) includes a tent for the party.*
