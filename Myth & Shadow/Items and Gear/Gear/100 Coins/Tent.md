# Tent

Holds all the PCs (~6).

Without a tent, the PCs do not remove [Exhausted](../../../Game%20Procedures/Conditions/Exhausted.md) when they [Long Rest](../../../Game%20Procedures/Core%20Procedures/Resting.md#Long%20Rest) in the wilderness.

| ---------------- | ----- | ----- |
| 1                | 2     | 100   |
