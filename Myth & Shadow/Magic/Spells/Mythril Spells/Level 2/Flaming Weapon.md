# Flaming Weapon

| Level    | 2 #Spell2 |
| -------- | --------- |
| Casting  | 1 hour    |
| Duration | 1 hour    |
| Range    | Touch     |
| Domains  | #Fire     |

You touch one weapon wreathing it in flames. Damage done with this weapon deals an additional 1d4 [[../../../../Damage Types/Fire|Fire]] damage for the duration.


*Upcast: The damage increases by 1 per spell level.*
