# Levitate

| Level    | [2nd Level Spell](2nd%20Level%20Spells.md)          |
| -------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Casting  | 1 [Action](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Action.md) |
| Duration | 1 hour ([Concentration](../../../Spellcasting/Concentration.md)) |
| Range    | Touch                                               |
| Domains  | [Air](../../../Spell%20Domains/Air.md)              |

A target you touch becomes weightless for the duration. They can control their vertical height mentally, up to 30' feet on their [Turn](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Turn.md). They can push off of objects to move themselves further.

---
*Upcast: Lasts for an additional minute per spell level.*
