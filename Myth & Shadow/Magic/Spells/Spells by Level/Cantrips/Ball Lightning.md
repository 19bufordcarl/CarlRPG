# Ball Lightning

| Level    | [Cantrip]({Cantrips}.md)                                              |
| -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Casting  | 1 [Action](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Core%20Procedures/Action.md) |
| Duration | None                                                                  |
| Range    | 1 [Zone](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Core%20Procedures/Zone.md)     |

You hurl a small ball of lightning energy at a target within range.

The affected target makes a [Stamina](../../../../Player%20Characters/Attributes/Stamina.md) [Saving Throw](../../../Spellcasting/Saving%20Throw.md) or takes 1d12 [Tier 2](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Combat/Damage/Damage%20Tiers/Tier%202.md) [Lightning](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Combat/Damage/Damage%20Types/Lightning.md) damage.

---
*Upgrade: Deal 1d12 additional damage per [Arcana](../../../../Player%20Characters/Skills/Arcana.md) proficiency tier above 1.*
