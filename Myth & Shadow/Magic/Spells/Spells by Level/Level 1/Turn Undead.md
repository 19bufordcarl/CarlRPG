# Turn Undead

| Level    | [1st Level Spell](1st%20Level%20Spells.md)                            |
| -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Casting  | 1 [Action](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Core%20Procedures/Action.md) |
| Duration | Until dispelled ([Concentration](../../Concentration.md))             |
| Range    | Self                                                                  |
| Domains  | [Life](../../Spell%20Domains/Life.md)                                 |

You sanctify the area around you for the duration.

Create a 30 foot [Aura](../../Areas%20of%20Effect/Aura.md) centered on you.

Any affected [Undead](../../../../Resources%20for%20GMs/Creatures/Creature%20Types/Undead.md) whose [Level](../../../../Player%20Characters/Derived%20Statistics/Level.md) is less than the [Spell Level](../../Spell%20Level.md) of this spell are destroyed.

All other affected [Undead](../../../../Resources%20for%20GMs/Creatures/Creature%20Types/Undead.md) are [Blinded](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Conditions/Blinded.md) and [Deafened](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Conditions/Deafened.md). They may make a [DC](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Core%20Procedures/DC.md) 12 [Physical Defense](../../../../Player%20Characters/Derived%20Statistics/Physical%20Defense.md) [Check](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Core%20Procedures/Check.md) to [Resist](../../Resist.md) this spell once per [Round](../../../../Game%20Procedures/Core%20Procedures/Round.md).

---
*Upcast: Increase the DC by 2 per spell level.*
