## Attacks

Can make three Deathly Claws attacks with 1 [Action](../../../Game%20Procedures/Action.md).

- Deathly Claws
	- +6 to [Melee Attack](../../../Game%20Procedures/Melee%20Attack.md) rolls
	- d12 [Necrotic](../../../Damage%20Types/Necrotic.md) damage
- Life Drain
	- Applied on Deathly Claws hits
	- 1 *temporary* [Constitution](../../../Player%20Characters/Chosen%20Statistics/Constitution.md) damage (-5 [Constitution](../../../Player%20Characters/Chosen%20Statistics/Constitution.md) is instant death)
