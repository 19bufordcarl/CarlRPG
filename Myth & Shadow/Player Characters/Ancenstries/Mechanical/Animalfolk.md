# Animalfolk

Animalfolk ancestries are [Humanoids](../../../Resources%20for%20GMs/Creatures/Creature%20Types/Humanoid.md) who resemble animals. They are typically highly adapted to the environment of their ancestral homeland. They have the [Adaptive](#Adaptive) feature.
