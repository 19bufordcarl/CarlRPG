# Versatile

A Versatile means the weapon is wielded with one handed by default, but if wielded with two hands you may increase the damage die by one step. See [Two Handed Property](Two%20Handed%20Property.md).

## Damage Die Steps


| Damage Step | Die   |
| ----------: | ----- |
|           1 | d4    |
|           2 | d6    |
|           3 | d8    |
|           4 | d10   |
|           5 | d12   |
|           6 | d12+1 |
|           7 | etc.  |
