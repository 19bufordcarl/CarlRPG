# Delver's Kit

A delver's kit costs 50 coins, and every new character can start with one. It contains:

| Item                 | Quantity | Item Slots        | Price |
| -------------------- | -------- | ----------------- | ----- |
| Backpack & Waterskin | 1        | 0 (First is Free) | 25    |
| Flint and Steel      | 1        | 1                 | 5     |
| Torches              | 2        | 2                 | 10    |
| Rations              | 2        | 1                 | 5     |
| Rope, 50'            | 1        | 1                 | 5     |
